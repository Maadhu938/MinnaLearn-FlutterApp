Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

MinnaLearn is a Japanese language learning app developed to help users learn Japanese at the JLPT N5 level. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Data We Collect

MinnaLearn may collect and store the following information:

  • Your name and email address (via Firebase Authentication, including Google Sign-In)
  • Study progress, including lesson completion, study time, streaks, quiz scores, bookmarked vocabulary, learned kanji, and achievements
  • All progress data is stored locally on your device using SQLite and synced to Firebase Cloud Firestore if you are signed in

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Track your learning progress and streaks
  • Save and sync your data across devices using Firebase Cloud Firestore
  • Send local study reminders and notifications (with your permission)

3. Data Sharing

  • Your data is shared with Firebase (Google) for authentication and cloud sync
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any other third parties
  • No data is used for advertising or profiling purposes

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Your study data is stored locally on your device using SQLite
  • When signed in, your data is synced to Google Firebase (Firestore)
  • Firebase provides industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest
  • You can use the app offline without signing in; all data stays on your device

5. Third-Party Services

MinnaLearn uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) - for sign-in functionality
  • Firebase Cloud Firestore - for cloud sync of your progress

These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app
  • Request correction of your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data from the app settings (Profile → Delete Account)
  • Sign out at any time

For more details, see our Account & Data Deletion page.

7. Children's Privacy

MinnaLearn does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
